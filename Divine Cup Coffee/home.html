Divine Cup Coffee

Behind every successful person is a divine cup of coffee.

Direct Trade

At Divine we proudly serve Intelligentsia Direct Trade coffee. We believe in coffee quality and have made a commitment to our customers to offer only truly dazzling Specialty Coffees that speak for themselves in the cup. We believe that to get such coffees we need to work closely with actual producers, not just importers or exporters, so that we can build great coffees from the very start.

Organic

We do our best to source the very finest local, organic ingredients. We partner with Cruze Dairy Farms to ensure sure all of our milk based beverages are made with fresh, organic milk from happy, grass fed cows right here in East Tennessee. From baked goods to chocolate, our buying decisions support farmers, co-ops, and local companies that deliver rich, quality ingredients.

In Season

Coffee is just like great fruit and vegetables. Most coffee-producing countries have only one specific harvest season each year, and once the coffee is picked from the tree, it begins the inevitable process of slow decline, losing quality with the passage of time. At Divine our coffees are offered only while they are fresh and retain the vibrancy that both nature and the coffee farmer intended.
